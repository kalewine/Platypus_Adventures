YOGA

Platypus Yoga is Yoga for EVERY BODY! Our program combines the principles of ancient yoga practices with a fun and engaging twist, which is coupled with fun music. Classes take place at Surya Yoga Studio in Simpsonville on Saturdays from 2-3 pm. All classes are adapted for all abilities and geared for individuals ages 5-12.
