TRI MY WAY

An Adaptive Triathalon

Tri My Way is a swim/bike/run event for adults ages 19 and up with disabilities including Autism, Cerebral Palsy, Muscular Dystrophy, etc. ALL abilities are welcomed, including those that may require the use of flotation devices, adapted bikes, walkers, power wheelchairs, and other assistive devices. All athletes are paired with volunteers to encourage, assist, and ensure safety.

Benefits of Triathalon Training:

  • Improved Cardiovascular Health
    Swimming, biking, and running (or adapted alternatives) enhance heart and lung function.
  • Increased Strength & Endurance
    Engaging multiple muscle groups builds overall strength, balance, and stamina.
  • Enhanced Mobility & Flexibility
    Regular movement can help maintain or improve range of motion and coordination.
  • Boosted Confidence & Independence
    Achieving training milestones fosters a sense of personal accomplishment.
  • Stress Reduction & Mental Clarity
    Physical activity releases endorphins, improving mood and reducing anxiety.
  • Resilience & Goal Setting
    Training encourages perseverance, problem-solving, and self-discipline.
  • Connection & Camaraderie
    Participating in adaptive sports builds friendships and a supportive community.
  • Inspiration & Advocacy
    Competing challenges stereotypes and inspires others to embrace active lifestyles.
