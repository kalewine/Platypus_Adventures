Platypus Adventures Mission Statement

Platypus Adventures is taking steps towards inclusion by creating adaptive opportunities for individuals of different abilities. We strive to promote safe and nurturing opportunities to facilitate growth physically, emotionally, cognitively, and socially. We are building community, courage, and confidence one step at a time.

We seek to...

Build confidence

Promote and support healthy lifestyles

Increase visibility within the community

Build lasting relationships within the community

Provide a stepping stone to participation in non-adaptive events
