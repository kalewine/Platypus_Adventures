Tri My Best Triathalon

Swim, Bike, and Run to the finishline in this adaptive adventure. With buddies on both sides and a community of people cheering along the way, youth of all abilities can gain a sense of pride and accomplishment by participating in our annual Tri My Best Triathalon.

Platypus Yoga

Ground your body in tree pose and experience the comfort of curling up into child's pose during Platypus Yoga. Adapted to fit the needs of every body, Platypus Yoga blends traditional yoga poses with platypus fun to provide a kid friendly yoga experience, good for the whole family.
