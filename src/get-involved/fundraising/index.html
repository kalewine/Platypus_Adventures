Play, Drink, Give
An Adult Field Day Fundraiser

Fall 2022

Play, Drink, Give is an Adult Field Day fundraiser benefiting The Little White House and Platypus Adventures. This event is an adults only (21+) competition with a variety of different challenges. Teams will play and have fun, remembering what it means to be a carefree kid. Participants are sure to relive the glory days of elementary school Field Day with a little friendly competition!
