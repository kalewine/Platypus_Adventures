Super Hero 5K

April 29, 2023

The Super Hero 5K on April 29th is hosted by the Fountain Inn Police Department. Learn more on their website. It benefits three local non-profits.

  • The Little White House is dedicated to creating a safe and loving place for children on the autism spectrum to be equipped for day to day life.
  • The Selah Grey Foundation exists to advocate, educate, support, and counsel families that have suffered traumatic experiences or have children with special needs.
  • Platypus Adventures creates adaptive athletic opportunities for individuals with disabilities, building community, courage, and confidence one step at a time.
