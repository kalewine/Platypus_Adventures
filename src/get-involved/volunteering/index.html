What to Expect as a Volunteer

We want both our athletes and volunteers to have a positive experience. Safety is a core value for PA. Training will vary depending on the volunteer position. Alison and Melissa are always available and strive to communicate clearly with our volunteers and families.

Other Opportunities

We also need volunteers at other events and fundraisers and help organizing these events. If you would like to work on the administrative side, please contact us directly. We will find an area you can volunteer.
